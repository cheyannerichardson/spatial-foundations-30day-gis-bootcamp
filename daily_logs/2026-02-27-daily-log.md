# GitHub Daily Log - February 27, 2026

## Decision Matrix Development — Coastal Heritage Resilience Analysis

###### I began by developing an **Excel Decision Matrix** to assess and prioritize five lighthouse restoration projects as part of a **coastal heritage resilience analysis**. This matrix was built from a **custom template I personally designed**, giving me full control over its structure, evaluation criteria, and scoring logic.  

###### I replaced the original total formula with a **weighted total formula**, improving accuracy and fairness by allowing each criterion to contribute proportionally to the overall project score. To deepen the analysis, I created **four analytical scenarios** that test project viability under varying weighting priorities and environmental conditions. These scenarios reveal how shifts—such as prioritizing **cost efficiency** versus **cultural or climate resilience**—influence project rankings and final recommendations.

    The scenarios include:
    Base Scenario: Balanced weighting across all criteria.  
    Budget Crisis: Emphasizes cost efficiency and minimal restoration cost.  
    History Focus: Prioritizes historical and cultural significance.  
    Climate Emergency: Elevates climate vulnerability and adaptation readiness.  
    Tourism Focus: Highlights visitor potential and socioeconomic benefits.  


###### I developed a new **Dashboard sheet** titled *Lighthouse Restoration Priority Dashboard* that consolidates all scenario outputs. The dashboard includes:

    A color‑coded overall CVI (Coastal Vulnerability Index) risk level:  
        🟥 Critical  
        🟧 High  
        🟨 Medium  
        🟩 Low  
    A top 3 priority ranking for each scenario, displaying the leading lighthouse projects by weighted score.
    A **bar chart** visualizing total CVI need per lighthouse, showing comparative vulnerability.
    A Lighthouse Restoration Priority Matrix illustrating which lighthouses should be prioritized by percentage.
